Notes:

Relative put price data will be stale on trading holidays. In addition, data collected on the third Friday of any month will use the last price, instead of the bid-ask midpoint.

Calculation takes the average relative price if multiple options are tied.

The same option is never used for multiple calculations on the same day (ties are broken arbitrarily).
